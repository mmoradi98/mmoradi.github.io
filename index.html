About me

Born in LA. Training in New York.

Hi, I'm Michael — a first-year PM&R resident at Westchester Medical Center. I grew up in Los Angeles and completed my undergraduate studies at UCLA, where I earned a B.S. in Psychobiology. I then moved across the country for medical school at Drexel University College of Medicine, graduating in 2025.

Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation drew me in because of its unique focus on function, recovery, and the whole patient. I'm passionate about helping people rebuild their lives after injury or illness — whether that's a stroke survivor relearning to walk or an athlete returning to sport.

Outside the hospital, you'll find me on the basketball or soccer court, rooting for the Lakers, or out on the trails.
